Frequently Asked Questions

Is the website really free?

Yes! All design and development work is completely free. You only pay for hosting and maintenance services ($97/month).

What's included in hosting & maintenance?

Hosting includes professional web hosting, SSL certificate, regular backups, security updates, technical support, and content updates.

How long does it take to build?

Typically 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your website and how quickly you provide content and feedback.

Can I cancel anytime?

We require a 12-month minimum commitment for hosting and maintenance. After that, you can cancel with 30 days notice.

Do I own the website?

Yes! Once the website is built and launched, you own all the design and content. You can take it elsewhere if needed. However, if you move it within the 12-month minimum commitment period, there is a $750 repackaging fee.

What if I need changes later?

Minor content updates are included in the monthly maintenance fee. Major redesigns or new features can be discussed separately.

Can I move my website elsewhere?

Yes, you own the website. However, if you move it elsewhere within the 12-month minimum commitment period, there is a $750 repackaging fee to transfer everything to your new hosting provider.
